The Commercial
Intelligence Framework

A repeatable system for decoding any business model. Strip away the surface, identify who holds the power, map the loop, and find the gap. Applies to every industry without exception.

The Foundation — Start Here Every Time
Everyone is a buyer and a seller.
Power belongs to whoever is selling the scarcer thing.
This is the root of every model. Every relationship, every transaction, every ecosystem sits on top of this truth. Before anything else, ask: what is each party buying and what are they selling?
The Seven Steps
01
Start With the Outcome
What is the end goal of this entire ecosystem? Sell the house. Fill the stadium. Sign the player. Win the league. Everything else maps back to this single outcome. Define it clearly before anything else.
→ What does success look like for the entire ecosystem?
02
List Every Party Involved
Map everyone directly and indirectly involved. The obvious ones and the hidden ones. No ecosystem is as simple as it first appears. The hidden parties often hold more power than the visible ones.
→ Who else is in the room even when they're not in the room?
03
Identify the Real ICP
Not who the business claims to serve. Who does the entire model actually depend on to function? In football it is the player not the fan. In property media it is the developer not the homeowner. The stated customer and the real customer are often different.
→ Who does the loop collapse without?
04
Follow the Money Backwards
Ignore the obvious revenue stream. Find the one the whole machine is actually built around. Surface revenue is rarely the real revenue. Follow it backwards until you find the engine that everything else is protecting.
→ What would break the business if it stopped?
05
Find the Multiplier Asset
What single thing generates return across multiple revenue streams simultaneously? The elite footballer triggers shirt sales, broadcast value, social growth and sponsorships all at once. One asset, multiple streams. Find it in every model you decode.
→ What one thing makes everything else worth more?
06
Map the Loop
Every sustainable model has a self reinforcing cycle. No loop means no compounding, no defensibility, no moat. Map every party and how they feed each other. Club attracts player, player attracts fans, fans attract money, money attracts better players.
→ What feeds what and in what order?
07
Spot the Proof of Concept Layer
What does each party need to see to trust the model enough to participate? Every party has a different proof requirement. Find what each side needs to see and you know exactly how to position yourself to enter or exploit the ecosystem.
→ What does each side need to see before they commit?
+
The Social Cues Layer
Every ecosystem has unwritten rules about status, trust and belonging. These cues reveal what each party actually values beneath the surface. Sometimes the currency is money. Sometimes credibility, association or access. Read the cues, understand the real currency of that world.
→ What signals status, safety and belonging in this world?

The Full Circle Moment
Step 01
What They Think About
Identify the primary driver of your ICP. What occupies their mind. What they want more than anything else in this context. That is the lens everything else gets filtered through.
Step 02
What They Notice
Whatever they think about is what they notice. Their attention filters for proof of their desired outcome. They only see what confirms their goal. Everything else is invisible to them.
Step 03
How You Move Them
Whatever they notice is what influences them. Become the proof of concept for what they want and you have their attention. Repeat from every angle. That is how you sell to every party in the ecosystem.
Real World Decoded — Examples
Football Club
How the model actually works — not how most people think it works.
Real ICP
  • The elite player — not the fan
  • Without the player the loop collapses
  • Fan loyalty is downstream of player attachment
Real Revenue
  • Broadcast deals — not ticket sales
  • Shirt sponsorship and global brand deals
  • Digital audience monetisation
Multiplier Asset
  • The iconic player with a global audience
  • Triggers shirts, broadcast, social, sponsorship
  • ROI can be 4–5x wages monthly
The Loop
  • Club attracts player via stadium and network
  • Player attracts fans via lifestyle and fame
  • Fans attract broadcasters and sponsors
  • Money attracts the next elite player
The club is not a football business. It is a media company that happens to play football. The sport is the content. The players are the talent. The fans are the audience sold to advertisers.
Property Staging Company
Most staging companies target homeowners. That is the wrong ICP.
Real ICP
  • Property developer — not homeowner
  • Developers need empty spaces staged for lifestyle effect
  • Estate agents are the connector not the customer
Proof of Concept
  • Portfolio of completed high-end developments
  • Premium visual presence — website, materials
  • Streamlined process and delivery consistency
Power Holder
  • The developer holds all the leverage
  • They control the volume and the brief
  • Everything else orbits winning and keeping them
The Loop
  • Staging company wins developer trust
  • Well staged property sells faster
  • Agent looks good to developer
  • Developer trusts agent with next plot
Markeb Media — Our Own Model Decoded
The same framework applied to ourselves — two separate ICPs, one business serving both.
Agent ICP
  • Fast, high-quality, consistent media
  • Driver: credibility with vendors
  • Proof: portfolio quality and 48hr turnaround
  • Media is the entry point — process closes the deal
Developer ICP
  • Full campaign thinking and brand narrative
  • Driver: sales velocity and project prestige
  • Proof: Northstone case study and premium output
  • Media is the door — full service is the ceiling
Multiplier Asset
  • Great media opens both audiences simultaneously
  • One shoot generates agent loyalty and developer proof
  • Case study content feeds sales, marketing, and trust
Our Loop
  • Great media attracts agents
  • Agents introduce developers
  • Developers need full service
  • Full service creates case studies that attract more agents
Media is our acquisition tool and our proof of concept for both audiences. Our team is our retention and upsell infrastructure. Two ICPs, one business, one loop.
