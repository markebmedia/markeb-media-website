Stage 3 — The meeting
Every meeting follows the same structure. This is where revenue is made.
Open with questions — listen first
"What does your current marketing look like?"  ·  "Are you happy with how your listings perform?"  ·  "What's your biggest challenge with content right now?"  ·  "Are you building your personal brand alongside listings?"
Recommend the right service — don't present everything
Match the service to what they told you. Lead the decision confidently. Refer to the Service Prices tab for reference.
Sign them up to the dashboard — in the meeting
Walk through the platform live. Sign up before they leave. A client who leaves without an account is a warm lead, not a client.
Confirm the first shoot
Every meeting ends with either a confirmed booking or a scheduled follow-up with a clear next step. Never leave without one.

Endorsement

The goal: Secure a relationship with a regional director of a brokerage network (eXp, KW, The Agency UK, Moveli, etc.) so they endorse Markeb Media to all agents under their umbrella. One director relationship can unlock 20–200 agents in a single conversation. The route in is always a warm introduction from one of our existing personal agent clients inside that brokerage.

What we offer the director: A discounted personal branding session (photography and/or video) as a goodwill gesture. This makes the introduction feel reciprocal and gives them a personal reason to promote us. The message is: we want to help their agents improve output, quality, and consistency — and help the director improve their own professional image and self-image at the same time.

Head of Operations Executive Assistant
Stage 1 — Ask an existing client for a warm introduction
The warm intro is everything. Identify which of your existing personal agent clients is closest to their regional director or market centre leader. HoO or EA makes the ask personally — not by bulk message.
Identify which existing client is closest to their director
Look at your existing 17 clients. Which brokerage are they in? Who in that brokerage are they likely to know well? Check their Instagram — do they follow or engage with the regional director? Ask casually: "Do you know [Director Name] at all?"
HoO or EA: Ask the client for the introduction
"You've been a brilliant client and we're looking to grow our presence across [Brokerage]. Would you be happy to introduce us to [Director Name]? We'd love to show them how we can help the agents in your network — and we'd take care of a personal branding session for them as a thank you for their time."
Make the ask low-friction. A simple mention on WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or in their next conversation is enough. Don't ask for a formal email.
