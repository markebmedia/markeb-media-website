Frequently Asked Questions

Questions?
We've got answers.

How quickly can you schedule a shoot?

We typically have availability within 2 to 3 days. For urgent requirements, we can often accommodate same-day or next-day bookings depending on location and weather conditions.

What's included in the 48-hour delivery?

Professionally edited photos, walkthrough videos, drone footage where applicable, and all content optimised and sized for Rightmove, Zoopla, and social media platforms.

Do you cover my area?

We serve estate agents throughout the UK across 8 regions. Travel costs are included in standard pricing for most locations. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific area.

What if the weather is bad on shoot day?

We monitor conditions closely and will reschedule drone work if necessary. Interior photography and videography can proceed regardless of weather.

How does content tracking work?

Your client portal provides real-time updates on your project from shoot completion through editing to final delivery. You always know exactly where your content stands.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment terms for regular clients and can discuss payment plans for larger projects or ongoing partnerships.

Can I get the raw, unedited files?

Raw files can be provided upon request for an additional fee. Our edited versions are specifically produced for maximum marketing impact across all platforms.

What equipment do you use?

We use professional-grade cameras, stabilised gimbals, and CAA-approved drones. All equipment is regularly maintained to ensure consistently high-quality results on every shoot.

Get in touch and we will provide personalised answers for your requirements.
