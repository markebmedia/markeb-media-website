Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about working with Markeb Media

How quickly can you schedule a shoot?

+
We typically have availability within 2-3 days. For urgent requirements, we can often accommodate same-day or next-day bookings depending on location and weather conditions.

What's included in the 48-hour delivery?

+
You'll receive professionally edited photos, walkthrough videos, drone footage (if applicable), and all content optimised for Rightmove, Zoopla, and social media platforms. Everything is delivered via our secure client portal.

Do you cover my area?

+
We serve estate agents throughout the UK. Travel costs are included in our standard pricing for most locations. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific area.

What if the weather is bad on shoot day?

+
We monitor weather conditions closely and will reschedule drone work if necessary. Interior photography and videography can proceed regardless of weather, ensuring minimal delays to your marketing timeline.

How does the content tracking work?

+
Our client portal provides real-time updates on your project status - from shoot completion to editing stages and final delivery. You'll always know exactly where your content stands.

Can I get the raw, unedited files?

+
We provide professionally edited content optimised for marketing use. Raw files can be provided upon request for an additional fee, though our edited versions are specifically designed for maximum marketing impact.

Do you offer payment plans?

+
Yes, we understand cash flow is important for estate agents. We offer flexible payment terms for regular clients and can discuss payment plans for larger projects or ongoing partnerships.

What equipment do you use?

+
We use professional-grade cameras, stabilised gimbals, and CAA-approved drones. All equipment is regularly maintained and updated to ensure consistently high-quality results for every shoot.
